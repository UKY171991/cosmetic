Welcome to COSGYNAE 2025

It is our proud privilege to invite you to the third it's kind unique academic feast on Cosmetic Gynaecology. Live workshop and Scientific conference “Cosgynae 2025” will be held on August 14 - 16, 2025 at Chandigarh, India in which more than 60 National and International speakers will give informative talks on relevant topics.

This  Mega International conference is organized under the aegis of the Aesthetic Regenerative Gynecology Society ( InSARG), Mohali Obstetrics & Gynaecology Society (MOHOGS)  in association with the Vascular Society for Limb Salvage, Indian academy of cosmetic dermatology and gynecology (IACDG) and The Touch clinic (Advanced IVF , Cosmetic Gynae & High Risk pregnancy center).

The aim is to disseminate knowledge, foster research and innovation, advance education, training and encourage multidisciplinary collaboration in Women Cosmetic Health. The event will have world level, highly cited class plenary speakers in addition to workshops and special sessions.
