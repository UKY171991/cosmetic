Welcome to COSGYNAE 2025

It is our proud privilege to invite you to the third it’s kind unique academic feast on cosmetic gynecology and the latest advances in aesthetics, general obstetrics, gynecology, and infertility plus the annual Insarg conference with certified workshop and scientific conference cosgynae25 will be held on August 14-16, 2025 at Chandigarh, India in which more than 50 national and international speakers will give informative talks on relevant topics. This time we are adding unique certified workshops to train all aspiring gynecologists.


This  Mega International conference is organized under the aegis of the Aesthetic Regenerative Gynecology Society ( InSARG), Mohali Obstetrics & Gynaecology Society (MOHOGS)  in association with the Vascular Society for Limb Salvage, The Touch clinic (Advanced IVF , Cosmetic Gynae & High Risk pregnancy center) .

The aim of "COSGYNAE 2025 is to disseminate knowledge, foster research and innovation, advance education, training and encourage multidisciplinary collaboration in Women Cosmetic Health. The event will have world level, highly cited class plenary speakers in addition to workshops and special sessions.

It will be a great opportunity for the newer entrants to learn these advanced techniques and at the same time the conference will be a great platform for established ones to update their knowledge and hone their skills to even more perfection.

Chandigarh, also known as “City Beautiful”, is picturesquely located at the foothills of Shivaliks. It is considered as one of the best experiments in urban planning and modern architecture in India. The city has several tourist attractions like Rock Garden, Sukhna Lake, Rose Garden and is in close proximity to several other tourist spots like Shimla, Kasauli, Parwanoo, Manali etc.

We look forward welcoming you all to the City Beautiful and urge you to mark the dates.
